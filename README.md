This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of the cryptocurrency market from April 2013 to July 2021. The dashboard is designed to help investors and enthusiasts visualize market trends, track volatility, and compare the performance of top cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Tether, BNB, and Cardano) over time.

The goal of this project was to transform raw historical price data into actionable insights using advanced Data Modeling and DAX.

üöÄ Key Features
Market Overview: KPI cards displaying current Price, Market Cap, and 24h Volume.

Trend Analysis: Interactive line charts visualizing historical price movements and moving averages.

Coin Comparison: Slicers and filters allowing users to compare specific coins against one another.

Volatility Tracking: Visuals highlighting daily percentage changes and highs/lows.

üí° Key Insights Derived
Based on the analysis of the dataset, the following insights were uncovered:


Market Trends: Bitcoin (BTC) demonstrated a massive Bullish trend over the analyzed period, starting at ~$144 in 2013 and closing near $34,000 in July 2021. It reached an All-Time High (ATH) of **$64,863** on April 14, 2021.



Asset Performance (ROI): While Bitcoin is the market leader, Ethereum (ETH) showed the highest growth potential with an ROI of over 308,000%, closely followed by BNB (~305,000%).



Market Dominance: Bitcoin retains a significant market share, holding 51.3% dominance among the top 5 assets, followed by Ethereum at 21.7%.



Volatility: Among the top 5 coins by market cap, Cardano (ADA) exhibited the highest volatility (daily fluctuation standard deviation of 8.34%), indicating a higher risk/reward profile compared to Bitcoin.



Volume Spikes: The highest trading volume across the market was recorded on February 26, 2021, coinciding with significant market corrections and activity.
